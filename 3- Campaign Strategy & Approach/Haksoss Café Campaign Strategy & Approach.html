Campaign Strategy & Approach

The strategy for Haksoss Café was designed to transform broad luxury positioning goals into a structured, data-driven campaign focused on measurable engagement, loyalty growth, and ROI optimization.

Primary Objective

Position Haksoss Café as Alexandria’s top luxury morning destination while building a loyal, high-LTV customer base.

Target Audience

Affluent morning coffee enthusiasts aged 20–50, highly active on Instagram and Facebook, who value premium experiences and cultural authenticity.

Channels

Social Media, Email CRM, SEO/SEM, and in-store experiences to maximize awareness, conversions, and retention.

Campaign Design

10-step automation journey from awareness to retention, with creative A/B testing and measurable KPI tracking.
