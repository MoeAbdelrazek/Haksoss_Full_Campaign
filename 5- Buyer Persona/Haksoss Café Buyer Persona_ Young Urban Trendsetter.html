Buyer Persona — Young Urban Trendsetter

Based on comprehensive market and behavioral data, the “Young Urban Trendsetter” represents Haksoss Café’s most socially influential audience — individuals who seek premium, shareable experiences that reinforce their personal brand of sophistication and trend awareness.

Demographics

Age: 20–50
Gender: 50% Male / 50% Female
Income: EGP 200k–1M+
Location: Urban Alexandria
Occupation: Students & Professionals

Psychographics

Trend-focused, socially expressive, digitally connected. Values aesthetics, recognition, and lifestyle validation through experiences.

Goals & Motivations

Share Instagram-worthy experiences, attend exclusive events, and maintain recognition as early adopters in their social circles.

Challenges

High demand for uniqueness, limited time and budget, saturation of similar café experiences, and need for seamless service.

Preferred Channels

Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, lifestyle influencers, and email-based loyalty campaigns.

Loyalty Behavior

Engages through app-based referrals, posts experiences online, and maintains repeat visits through VIP or exclusive reward programs.
