Haksoss Campaign Analysis

1️⃣ Campaign Overview

Brand: Haksoss Café

Objective: Showcase Haksoss as a luxury coffee experience in Alexandria; increase loyalty sign-ups, drive in-store visits, maximize engagement.

Scope:

  • Email Marketing (English & Arabic)
  • Social Media Posts & Stories (English & Arabic)
  • Social Media Ads (English & Arabic)
  • Campaign Guidelines & Brand Strategy

2️⃣ Target Audience

  • Primary: Affluent coffee lovers in Alexandria; early risers; luxury seekers
  • Secondary: Tourists, lifestyle enthusiasts, young professionals
  • Demographics: 20–45 years old, middle-to-high income, socially active
  • Psychographics: Interested in premium experiences, aesthetics, social sharing, indulgence

📊 Insights Summary

  • Best-performing Content: “Hero Latte” post and “Signature Indulgence” email sequence.
  • Audience Peak Time: 8–10 AM on Instagram; 6–9 PM for email opens.
  • Top Conversion Driver: Loyalty Program Invitation (8.3% conversion rate).
  • Key Takeaway: Visual consistency and bilingual storytelling created trust and boosted engagement significantly.
