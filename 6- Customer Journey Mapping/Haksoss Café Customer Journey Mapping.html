Customer Journey Mapping — Super Elite

Based on targeted research of high-net-worth clientele, the “Super Elite” persona represents Haksoss Café’s top-tier audience. Each stage of the journey is tailored for exclusivity, high-touch engagement, and premium experiences, maximizing lifetime value and advocacy.

Awareness

Touchpoints: Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, lifestyle blogs

Content: Hero visuals, influencer endorsements

Metrics: 80,800 unique users, 11.3% engagement

Strategic Reformulation: Focus on VIP audience via curated influencer partnerships and premium offers.

Consideration

Touchpoints: Social ads, email campaigns, landing pages

Content: VIP offers, curated menu highlights, testimonials

Metrics: CTR 3.8%, email open rate 35%

Strategic Reformulation: Highlight exclusivity and curated experiences to drive loyalty interest.

Conversion

Touchpoints: Loyalty program sign-up, in-store interactions

Content: Exclusive offers, VIP onboarding, premium coffee experience

Metrics: 1,070 loyalty sign-ups, 6.2% conversion rate, ROAS 4.11:1

Strategic Reformulation: Streamlined VIP onboarding with high-touch service and exclusive offers.

Retention

Touchpoints: Personalized email, SMS, VIP events, seasonal menus

Content: Seasonal offers, personalized recommendations, loyalty rewards

Metrics: 15% retention, 18% re-engagement

Strategic Reformulation: Hyper-personalized loyalty communications and exclusive events.

Advocacy

Touchpoints: Social sharing, referral programs, VIP testimonials

Content: Referral incentives, UGC campaigns, testimonials

Metrics: Positive reviews, referrals, social engagement growth

Strategic Reformulation: Activate VIP ambassadors with referral campaigns and recognition programs.

Methodology & Rationale

  • Primary Research: Focus groups, interviews, in-store observation targeting super-elite clientele.
  • Secondary Research: Social media trend analysis, competitor benchmarking, market reports.
  • Data Sources: Instagram/TikTok analytics, email metrics, loyalty program analytics, in-store footfall data.
  • Strategic Reformulation: Each stage redesigned for exclusivity, premium digital & in-store experiences, and high-touch engagement.

Key Insights

  • Awareness: Use high-quality visuals and curated influencer partnerships.
  • Consideration: Emphasize exclusivity and VIP-level benefits.
  • Conversion: Personalized experiences and VIP perks drive sign-ups.
  • Retention: Exclusive communications, seasonal menus, and VIP events.
  • Advocacy: Referral campaigns, UGC, and public recognition of VIP customers.
