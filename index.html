Core Strategy & Execution

Haksoss Café leveraged a **visual-first, bilingual campaign** centered on the signature Hero Latte, a comprehensive Chocolate Croissant offering, and rotating seasonal treats. The execution combined **Email Automation** (CRM), **Templated Social Creatives**, and targeted **Performance Ads** across Meta, Google Display, and TikTok. The primary conversion objective was loyalty signups, which drove a measurable in-store lift and achieved a strong **411% ROI**.

Measurable Objectives

  • **Brand Perception:** Build an emotional connection to morning rituals and Haksoss’ premium experience.
  • **Sales Uplift:** Increase morning footfall and online breakfast orders by **40–60%**.
  • **CRM Growth:** Acquire loyalty members to improve customer retention and repeat visits.
  • **Creative Excellence:** Showcase bilingual execution and professional, high-end creative assets.

Visual & Tone Guidelines

  • **Palette:** Modern minimal (White + Deep Blue + Soft Sand accents).
  • **Typography:** *Playfair Display* for headlines (Elegance), *Lato* for body copy (Clarity).
  • **Imagery:** Morning-light product photography with a focus on sensory appeal and cozy textures.
  • **Tone:** Elegant, sensory, and highly inviting, executed flawlessly in both English and Arabic.

Brand & Execution Governance

Visual Guidelines

Voice & Copy
