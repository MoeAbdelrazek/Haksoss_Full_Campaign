Case Study Overview

The “Wake Up With Luxury” campaign was designed to elevate Haksoss Café’s brand presence through a mix of paid media, personalized emails, and social storytelling. It leveraged Meta, Google Display, and TikTok Ads for awareness and conversions, paired with email and social media engagement for retention.
