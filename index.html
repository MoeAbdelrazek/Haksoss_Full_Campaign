Pillar 6: AI-Driven Hyper-Personalization

  • **Predictive CRM:** Mandate CRM integration with purchase history, time, and local weather to send personalized "Hero Latte" reminders, eliminating missed purchase opportunities.
  • **Dynamic Product Schema:** The website must automatically feature a **Focus Blend** (for weekday browsers) or a **Family Treat** (for weekend browsers) to maximize conversion per segment.

Pillar 7: The ESG Imperative

  • **Zero-Waste Storytelling:** Creative mandate to produce a "Sustainability Signature" asset—a short video showing the local sourcing of the coffee beans or the recycling process, linking the **Luxury Experience** to **Ethical Consumption.**
  • **Gamified Sustainability:** Loyalty program must offer **Bonus Points** for bringing their own reusable cup, driving customer behavior toward the brand's ethical mission.

  • **GMB Integration:** Post **"Hero Latte Upgrade"** offers weekly to boost local search rankings and connect online marketing to offline traffic.
  • **URLs:** All product URLs must be clean and keyword-rich: e.g., `/hero-latte`, `/seasonal-treats`.
