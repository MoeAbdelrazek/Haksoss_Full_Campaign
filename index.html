World-Class Strategic Focus

Pillar 1: Product Hegemony & The Sensory Signature

       

Top-tier campaigns own a sensory signature. Our strategy establishes the **Hero Latte** as the singular, irreplaceable core of the Haksoss experience, driving all content and sales.

       
         
           

The Irreplaceable Asset

           

We developed a **15-second 'Hero' video mandate** that focuses purely on the **sound and visual signature** of the latte (steam, first sip reaction) without naming the product. This creates an emotional hook that cannot be replicated by competitors.

         
         
           

Cultural Localization vs. Translation

           

All creative and copy was developed with an **Arab-first perspective**, hiring local Alexandrian talent to embody the "Luxury by the Sea" theme authentically. This ensured high engagement (11.3%) and resonance in the local market.

         
         
           

LTV Tiering & Community

           

Loyalty is framed as the **"Haksoss Morning Collective,"** shifting perception from points program to **exclusive membership.** Future strategy involves tiered access (**Gold Membership**) for VIP seasonal launch invites to boost LTV.
