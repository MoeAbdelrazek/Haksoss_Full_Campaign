Advanced Strategy & Learnings

Key technical and strategic implementations that led to the campaign's success.

  • **Hyper-Localized Targeting:** Geo-fencing targeted high-end residential and commercial zones in Alexandria's key districts (Smouha, Roushdy, Downtown), using customized Arabic/English creative variations.
  • **A/B Testing Matrix:** Three distinct email subject lines were A/B tested for each of the 6-step sequences, identifying sensory language (`Velvet, Aromatic`) and urgency (`Limited-time`) as the highest performers.
  • **Dynamic Ad Creative (DCO):** Ad copy dynamically inserted the viewer's estimated nearest neighborhood to personalize the call-to-action (`Visit Haksoss in [Neighborhood]`).
  • **Full-Funnel Measurement:** Implemented Server-Side Tracking (Conversions API) to accurately attribute POS (Point-of-Sale) loyalty sign-ups and in-store purchases back to the specific social ad campaign.
