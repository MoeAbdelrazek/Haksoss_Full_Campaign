Executive Summary

Haksoss Café executed a visual-first, bilingual campaign centered on the signature **Hero Latte** and **Chocolate Croissant**. The strategy integrated targeted ads across Meta and TikTok with a loyalty-driven email automation sequence, resulting in a measurable lift in morning footfall and a high return on investment.

Morning Awakening (1/6)

English

Advanced Strategy & AI-Driven Copywriting

This campaign leveraged generative AI tools for multi-language copy (English & Arabic) and A/B testing variations, optimizing emotional triggers and cultural relevance to maximize conversion.

Metric AI-Generated Copy (Arabic) Human-Written Copy (English) Improvement (AI over Human)
Avg. Open Rate 43.7% 42.2% **+1.5%**
Avg. CTR 6.05% 5.03% **+1.02%**
Avg. Conversion Rate 5.82% 4.48% **+1.34%**

Key Strategic Drivers:

  • **Sensory Language**: Focusing on the 'aroma' and 'velvety texture' of the coffee to elicit emotional response (Luxury Cafe Aesthetic).
  • **Bilingual Precision**: AI was used to ensure the Arabic copy used high-register, culturally resonant terms for 'Luxury' and 'Elegance', resulting in higher engagement.
  • **Loyalty Gating**: The high-value offer (Email 3) was intentionally coupled with the Loyalty CTA (Email 4) to convert short-term visitors into long-term members.
  • **Scarcity & Exclusivity**: All promotional materials used time-limited or VIP/Loyalty-gated offers to create urgency and reinforce the brand’s premium positioning.
