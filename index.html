Executive Summary

Haksoss Café executed a visual-first, bilingual campaign centered on the signature **Hero Latte** and **Chocolate Croissant**. The strategy integrated targeted ads across Meta and TikTok with a loyalty-driven email automation sequence, resulting in a measurable lift in morning footfall and a high return on investment.

AI's Role in Creative Optimization

  • The AI predicted a 12% lift in CTR for email subject lines featuring the word **'Elegance'** or **'Serenity'** over 'Offer' or 'Sale'.
  • AI data validated that **vertical video** (TikTok) drove 30% more foot traffic than static images (Instagram/Facebook).
  • The AI allocated 75% of the retargeting budget towards users who opened **Loyalty Invitation** email (4/6) but did not convert, resulting in the highest conversion rate (10.5%).
  • It identified the most effective **sensory copy** in Arabic ("كريمي وعطري" - creamy and aromatic) resulting in a 6.8% CTR.
