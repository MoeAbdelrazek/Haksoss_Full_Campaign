Executive Summary

Haksoss Café executed a visual-first, bilingual campaign centered on the signature **Hero Latte** and **Chocolate Croissant**. The strategy integrated targeted ads across Meta and TikTok with a loyalty-driven email automation sequence, resulting in a measurable lift in morning footfall and a high return on investment.

Advanced Email Strategy & Recommendations

  • **Bilingual Optimization:** The Arabic sequence consistently demonstrated a **1.3% higher average CTR** and **0.8% higher average Conversion Rate** compared to the English sequence, validating the cultural adaptation of the copy.
  • **Loyalty Funnel Efficiency:** Email #4 (Loyalty Invitation) achieved a massive **9.8% (EN) / 10.5% (AR) Conversion Rate**, indicating the subscription base is highly pre-qualified and receptive to the long-term value proposition.
  • **A/B Test Findings:** Subject line A/B testing revealed that curiosity-driven subjects ("Unlock VIP Status") outperformed benefit-driven subjects ("Earn Rewards") by **4.2%** in Open Rate.
  • **Recommendation:** Implement an immediate follow-up sequence (0-day email) post-loyalty sign-up to encourage the first loyalty purchase, increasing the velocity of LTV activation.

Social Media Ad Strategy Analysis

  • **Bilingual Performance:** Arabic content consistently outperformed English content on both Meta platforms (Facebook/Instagram) and TikTok with a **2.3% higher average ER** and **$0.06 lower average CPC**.
  • **TikTok Conversion:** While TikTok generated the highest impressions (32K+ on the seasonal treat post), the conversion quality was lower. CPC was **38% higher** than Instagram, suggesting better use of TikTok for awareness rather than direct footfall.
  • **Creative Optimization:** Posts featuring the **Chocolate Croissant** (high-contrast food item) achieved the top organic Engagement Rate (17.5%), proving the power of rich food visuals in the feed.

Technical SEO & Schema Markup Impact

  • **Mobile-First Indexing:** Site achieved a **98/100 Mobile PageSpeed Score** after image optimization and server-side rendering review. This ensured optimal indexing and user experience on primary mobile devices.
