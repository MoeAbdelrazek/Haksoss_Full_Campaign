Executive Summary

Haksoss Café executed a visual-first, bilingual campaign centered on the signature **Hero Latte** and **Chocolate Croissant**. The strategy integrated targeted ads across Meta and TikTok with a loyalty-driven email automation sequence, resulting in a measurable lift in morning footfall and a high return on investment.

Email Strategy and Key Takeaways: Bilingual Performance & SEO

The bilingual email strategy, deployed in both English and Arabic, focused on maximizing the average **Conversion Rate** by sequencing content from broad luxury appeal to specific, high-intent calls-to-action (Loyalty Program, Exclusive Offer). The Arabic sequence outperformed the English sequence in both average CTR and the final Loyalty Conversion Rate (11.1% vs 9.8%).

  • **Highest Open Rate:** Arabic Morning Awakening (47.5%) followed by English Morning Awakening (46.2%). *First email novelty works best*.
  • **Highest CTR:** Arabic Loyalty Invitation (7.8%) followed by English Loyalty Invitation (6.5%). *Subscribers are highly responsive to long-term value propositions*.
  • **Highest Conversion Rate:** Arabic Loyalty Invitation (11.1%) followed by English Loyalty Invitation (9.8%). *Direct conversion to the Loyalty Program was the most successful goal*.

Advanced Strategy: Leveraging Data, AI & Geographic Insights

The campaign utilized audience segmentation and dynamic ad creation tools to optimize targeting for luxury commuters and local premium clientele in Alexandria. Data from geographic targeting was key for future expansion.

Geographic Performance Breakdown

Targeted Zone (Alexandria) Reach (%) Conversion Rate (CR) CPA (EGP) Insight
Corniche/Sea View (Affluent) 45% 7.8% EGP 135 Highest intent and lowest CPA. Optimize budget here.
City Center (Business Commuters) 35% 5.9% EGP 155 Strong potential, needs a more aggressive "quick service" angle.
Residential Suburbs (Families) 20% 4.1% EGP 180 Lower CR/Higher CPA. Consider a family bundle offer.
  • **Geographic Hyper-Targeting**: Ads served exclusively within 5KM radius of the Alexandria Corniche and affluent residential areas during morning hours (7:00 AM – 11:00 AM).
  • **Creative A/B Testing**: Used automated tools to test English sensory copy vs. Arabic urgency-driven copy, leading to a higher budget allocation to the Arabic segment (60/40 split).
  • **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Modeling**: Loyalty sign-ups were identified as the highest-CLV segment, prompting a dedicated, high-conversion email flow for this audience.
  • **Product Focus**: Confirmed that the **Hero Latte + Chocolate Croissant** bundle drove the highest conversion rate (14.5% lift in bundle sales).
