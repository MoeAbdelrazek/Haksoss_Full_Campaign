Algorithmic Intelligence and Predictive Modeling 🧠 | Haksoss-ML V2

The campaign's superior 411% ROI was primarily driven by the deployment of a proprietary **Machine Learning Model** (Haksoss-ML V2). This model executed **Dynamic Creative Optimization (DCO)** and intelligent resource allocation, specifically designed to identify and target customers with the highest predicted Lifetime Value (LTV), thereby ensuring maximum efficiency and minimizing leakage.

Core ML Capabilities Deployed [SYSTEM MODULES]

  • Predictive LTV Scoring: The model ingests historical variables (purchase frequency, average basket size, and geo-location) to generate a precise LTV score for potential leads, prioritizing ad delivery to the top 20% of users with LTV scores > $60.
  • Dynamic Creative Optimization (DCO): Automated, real-time creative assembly based on user profiles. The model determined the best combination of assets (Latte vs Croissant), call-to-action (Sign-up vs Order Now), and language (English vs Arabic) to maximize conversion probability.
  • Budget Allocation Automation (BAA): Real-time, algorithmic shifting of ad spend between Meta and TikTok platforms based on hourly performance data, ensuring immediate response to diminishing returns and fluctuations in Cost Per Acquisition (CPA).
