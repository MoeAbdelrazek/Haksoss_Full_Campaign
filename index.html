Executive Summary

Haksoss Café leveraged a visual-first, bilingual campaign centered on the Hero Latte, a signature Chocolate Croissant, and rotating seasonal treats. The campaign combined email automation, templated social creatives, and targeted ads across Meta, Google Display and TikTok — all driving loyalty signups and measurable in-store lift.

Objectives

  • Build an emotional connection to morning rituals and Haksoss’ premium experience.
  • Increase morning footfall and online breakfast orders by 40–60%.
  • Acquire loyalty members and improve repeat visits.
  • Showcase bilingual execution and professional creative assets.

Creative Direction

  • Modern minimal palette: white + deep blue + soft sand accents.
  • Typography: Playfair Display for headlines, Lato for body copy.
  • Imagery: morning-light product photography; cozy textures.
  • Tone: Elegant, sensory, inviting.
