Haksoss Social Media Guidelines:

1. Tone: Elegant, luxurious, friendly.
2. Fonts: Playfair (headlines), Lato (body).
3. Colors: Deep-sea blue, sandy beige (campaign theme).
4. Posting Frequency: 1-2 posts/day on Instagram & Facebook, 3-5 stories/week.
5. Captions: Match the text files provided, include hashtags from hashtags_en.txt / hashtags_ar.txt.
6. Images: Always use templates from 1_Templates. Do not alter the templates.
7. Stories: Include swipe-up text where applicable.
8. Ads: Use captions from 4_Ads folder, paired with correct template.
